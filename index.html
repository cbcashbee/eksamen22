About me

The Future of Frontend

Half of the world’s population is female, yet the tech-industry is only consisting of 26.7% women in the field. This is where I want to be a change:

My name is Cecilie Bobjerg Cashbee, and I am a 24-year-old female, studying to becoming a multimedia designer.

With base in Odense, Denmark, I am currently studying at University College Lillebælt at my first semester. When I finish my studies, I would like to further educate myself, within my interest in both frontend development and -designing. Regards to my femininity, I see it only as a strength, as I will be able to provide a different perspective. There is a huge potential and lots of possibilities, for a female take on things, and I believe it is needed in the future development, of the tech-industry.
